--- layout: default title: "27 Point Buy Calculator" --- ## 27 Point Buy Calculator This interactive calculator lets you adjust six ability scores for your D&D character using the 27 Point Buy system. Each stat starts at 8 and can be raised to a maximum of 15 using plus and minus buttons. The cost follows standard D&D rules: going from 8 to 13 costs 1 point per increase, while increments from 13 to 15 cost 2 points each. The points remaining are calculated automatically.
