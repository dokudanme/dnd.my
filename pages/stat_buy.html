--- layout: default title: "27 Point Buy Calculator" --- ## 27 Point Buy Calculator This interactive calculator lets you adjust six ability scores for your D&D character using a 27 point buy system. Each stat starts at 8 and can be increased (up to 15) or decreased (down to 8) using the plus and minus buttons. The cost follows standard D&D 27 point buy rules: increasing from 8 to any score up to 13 costs 1 point per increment, and going from 13 to 14 (or 14 to 15) costs 2 points per increment.
